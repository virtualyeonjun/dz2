Hunting Adeline

Cat and Mouse Duet

The story concludes the narrative arc of Adeline Reilly and Zade Meadows.
Zade, a vigilante who leads an organization fighting human trafficking,
is desperately hunting for Adeline after she is abducted by "The Society",
a powerful trafficking organization.

The book alternates between Adeline's perspective as she endures torture and psychological manipulation during captivity,
and Zade's perspective as he ruthlessly tears apart the criminal underworld to find her.
Once rescued, Adeline, forever changed by her trauma, partners with Zade to seek vengeance,
transforming from a victim to an empowered force.
